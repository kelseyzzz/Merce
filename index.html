Mercé

At Mercé, we see that the world is unable to further withstand the strain that animal agriculture has put on it. In addition, we believe the suffering of the animals being raised for food has been hidden and ignored for too long. As a result, we have formed our entire identity around transparency. Our food is made without a single suffering animal as well as a far-less suffering earth. We have no secrets to hide. As the first restaurant to utilize clean-meat technology, Mercé is an entirely new creative experience that satisfies in an inspiring and guilt-free way.
